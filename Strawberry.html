Strawberry Milk Macarons

The filling of this macaron is infused with a flavorful strawberry jam. This dessert has a very strong strawberry flavor that is mildly tart and sweet. This can be enjoyed with a glass of strawberry milk or a light tea. The outside is crispy and the inside is soft and flavorful. It tastes just like a glass of strawberry milk in macaron form. Store in the refrigerator and take out for 10-15 minutes before serving. $24.00 for 10 macarons.
