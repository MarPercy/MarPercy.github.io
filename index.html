Caramel Macarons

Caramel Macarons-

This is the featured flavor of the week. This macaron has a crunchy exterior with a creamy filling and a drizzle of caramel in between. It has the perfect balance of sweet and creamy. It can be stored in the refrigerator for up to a week. Let it sit at room temperature for around 15- 20 minutes before serving or eating. Enjoy it with a hot cup of oolong tea. $8.00 for 5 macarons
