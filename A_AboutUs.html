About Us

We are a family run business here in Rockwall. We have dedicated a lot of time and effort into finding the most natural and high quality ingredients for our desserts. We use organic unbleached sugar and flour as well as cage-free eggs. Macaron Tea House is also eco-friendly as we try our best to use reusable piping bags and baking sheets. Even the dish soap used to wash the preparation bowls, are enviromentally safe and non-toxic. Our mission is to offer affordable desserts that are as natural and organic as possible in an eco-friendly and non-toxic environment. We hope to provide you with small treats and love from our family to yours. For any questions or special requests please send us an email, PM on Facebook, or DM on Instagram and we will respond as soon as possible.
